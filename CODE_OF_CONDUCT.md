# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of `mcp-data-br`, pledge to make
participation in our project a welcoming, harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the community.
- Showing patience with new contributors, including those for whom English
  or Portuguese is not their first language.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks, insulting or derogatory comments, and trolling.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (issues, pull
requests, discussions, and code review) and when an individual is
representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue
addressed to the maintainers or, for sensitive matters, by contacting the
repository owner ([@FilipePessoa30](https://github.com/FilipePessoa30))
directly through GitHub. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may take
corrective action — including editing, removing, or rejecting
contributions and comments that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct,
and temporarily or permanently banning contributors for behavior deemed
inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.
